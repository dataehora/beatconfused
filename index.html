  1. Set a BPM manually or use Tap Tempo to detect the pulse from your clicks or key presses.
  2. Choose the time signature and beat subdivision that fit your exercise, groove, or song.
  3. Pick a beat visual, tempo counting mode, sound style, and volume, then press Start.

Metronome FAQ

Can I use BeatConfused on mobile?

Yes. The interface is responsive and works well with touch controls, including tap tempo and the main transport button.

Why is vibration unavailable on some iPhones?

Many iPhone browsers do not expose the web vibration API, so BeatConfused shows the status automatically and keeps audio and visual cues available.

Which counting modes are available?

You can display beat count, measure plus beat, or switch counting off for a cleaner visual practice mode.
