Promenade prizefight

Two parody hopefuls have arrived in Clacton for a highly unserious clash. Press start and settle the argument with jumps, jabs, kicks, and grabs.

Controls

Player 1 · Bin Visage

  • A / D move
  • W jump

Player 2 · Ferry Rage

  • ← / → move
  • ↑ jump
  • K punch
  • L kick

How it plays

The fight runs for a single round. Every move has a short window and cooldown, so timing beats frantic button mashing. Win by knockout or by holding the lead when time expires.
