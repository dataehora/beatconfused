Session notes

Does this work well on mobile?

Yes. Controls are touch-friendly and tuned for quick rehearsal setup on phone, tablet, or desktop.

Why can haptics be unavailable on some iPhones?

Some iOS browsers do not expose the vibration API. When that happens, BeatConfused keeps clear audio and visual timing cues active.

Which counting modes are available?

Choose beat count, measure plus beat, or no count when you want a cleaner visual field.
